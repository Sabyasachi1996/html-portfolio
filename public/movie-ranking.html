The Best Movies According to Sunny

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

The dual character of a human being and showing that twist at the end is quite awesome to me.

The Usual Suspects

The incredible acting of Kevin Spacy, the whole plot to keep us all thinking about who the actual culprit is. And finally the twist at the end which will blow your mind!

Scarface

A simple story of Antonio Montana, an immigrant turned gangster/smuggler and an eventual fall of him due to his hunger of power and money.Awesome acting by Al Pacino.
